Welcome to the website of Chinese Language Teaching Development Centre!


The Centre is committed to promoting scholarly research on Chinese language teaching and learning, supporting the teaching of Chinese language at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. It is dedicated to creating a research platform to develop a promising research on Chinese language teaching and learning as a new frontier in tertiary education.
