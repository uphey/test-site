Seminars


8 March 2018

「文學中大」評審講座 － 遊走於評論和創作
從創作到評論，講者鄭政恆回顧十六年來的寫作歷程：自2002年起在詩刊發表作品，進而發表電影評論、文學評論和演藝評論，不同體裁的寫作在2002年的幾個月裡先後起步。講者除了就個人經歷和出版經歷作簡單分享外，也會緊貼當下的電影與文學等文化現象，跟觀眾一起面對和談論當下的社會文化。

Time: 2:30pm - 4:00pm
Venue: Learning Garden
Speaker: Matthew Cheng Ching-hang

Photos: https://www.chltcac.cuhk.edu.hk/activitiesreview


17 November 2017

「Aesthetic elements for a fine prose: talent, affection and humour
Prose is free in form. It resides next to one’s soul and mind. It is composed by the finest elements of talent, affection and humour that it is able to deliver an inspiring and refreshing aesthetic experience. Focusing on writing elements such as wisdom, talent and humour, this talk will share the secret of creating a moving atmosphere for prose through the sharing of speaker’s experience and the appreciation of classic works.

Time: 2:30pm - 4:00pm
Venue: University Bookstore, CUHK
Speaker: Mr. Choi Yick Wai (作家、文學評論家、現任香港作家聯會副會長，《香港作家》總編輯，筆名南山）
Language: Cantonese

Facebook: Unichinese
More details: https://www.chltcac.cuhk.edu.hk/activitiesreview


20 October 2017

Dialogue between the strait: Jargon translation
This workshop will compare translation differences between the strait through analysing a medical and a business technology report, and discuss the connection between translation and social language.

Time: 2:30pm - 4:00pm
Venue: The Learning Garden, University Library CUHK
Guest speaker: Mr. Michael Gold (Editor of The Economist）
Language: English and Mandarin

Facebook: Unichinese
Details: https://www.chltcac.cuhk.edu.hk/activitiesreview
